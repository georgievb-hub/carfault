Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-21

Overview

Car Fault is designed to help users identify vehicle issues by allowing them to upload images of dashboard warnings or describe their problems. The app does not require user accounts.

Data Collected

  • Images and descriptions: When you upload an image or provide a description, that content is processed to generate a diagnostic response. This content may be transmitted to servers or services that analyze the data.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics and may integrate Facebook SDK for marketing. These collect non-identifying usage data to help improve the app.
  • No Personal Data Required: The app does not require creation of user accounts or collection of personal data to function. You may optionally provide contact info when using feedback features.

How Data is Used

Uploaded images and descriptions are used to generate diagnostic responses. Analytics data helps us understand usage patterns and improve the Service.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Facebook) which have their own privacy practices. Please consult their policies for more details.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, though no system is entirely secure. If you have concerns, avoid uploading sensitive personal information.

Your Choices

You may opt out of analytics by disabling related settings on your device or within the app (when provided).
