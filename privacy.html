Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-21

Overview

Car Fault is designed to help users identify vehicle issues by allowing them to upload images of dashboard warnings or describe their problems. The app does not require user accounts.

Data Collected

  • Images and descriptions: Processed to generate a diagnostic response.
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics and optional Facebook SDK for non-identifying usage data.
  • No Personal Data Required: Accounts are not required; contact info is optional for feedback.

How Data is Used

Uploaded images and descriptions are used to generate diagnostic responses. Analytics data helps us understand usage patterns and improve the Service.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Facebook) with their own privacy practices. Consult their policies for details.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, though no system is entirely secure. Avoid uploading sensitive personal information if concerned.

Your Choices

You may opt out of analytics by disabling related settings on your device or within the app.
